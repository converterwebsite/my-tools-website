How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or Left/Right Buttons to move your spaceship
  • Press SPACEBAR or Shoot Button to fire bullets
  • Avoid enemy ships and their bullets
  • Destroy enemies to earn points
  • Collect power-ups for special abilities
  • Each level increases difficulty
